Principal lore of the game

Evil Shirly (Nemesis)

Through this character, the main story unfolds where the environment of the lore will be covered in an exploratory, intuitive and questioning way.

Without a doubt, this character is the one who has the best story development apart from his brother Zeke.

Zeke (Shirly's brother)

Through this character, the history of Aida's heirs and the history of Hykros and its existence as an organization are known.

Unfortunately, this character is not incorporated to be able to play it, but we will see it a lot in the story mode.

Secundary Missions

Where the history of secondary and tertiary characters is deepened, as well as the history of the world of Aesperia.

Series of missions where the rise of the characteristic characters (Simulacrum) of Tower of Fantasy is narrated.
